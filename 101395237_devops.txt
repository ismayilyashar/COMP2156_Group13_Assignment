Information about COMP2156 DevOps Class.


The COMP2156 course at George Brown College is part of the Computer Systems Technology Program (T147), 
and it focuses on DevOps for System Administration. This course is offered in the fourth semester of the program. 
The Computer Systems Technology program itself is a three-year program leading to an Ontario College Advanced Diploma, 
with specializations available in Networking and Systems in the third year. The course and program emphasize hands-on experience
in infrastructure virtualization, cloud computing, and best practices in IT, preparing students for various industry certifications.
