Some information about the importance of learning DevOps engineer in SDLC.

Learning DevOps in the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) is crucial for enhancing collaboration between development and 
operations teams, ensuring faster deployment of code with higher quality and reliability. DevOps practices integrate development and 
operations activities to reduce the time from development to operation, enabling continuous delivery and faster feedback loops. 
This approach fosters a culture of continuous improvement, reduces silos, increases efficiency, and 
improves the overall quality of software products. By adopting DevOps, organizations can better respond to market demands, 
improve customer satisfaction, and gain a competitive edge.
